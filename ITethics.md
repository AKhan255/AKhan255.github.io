#Ethics in IT

[Home](index.md) | [Ethics in IT](ITethics.md) | [Bird](bird.md)

Ethics in information technology is about using and creating technology in a responsible way. It means protecting people’s personal information, keeping systems secure, and making sure technology is fair and not harmful. Common issues include privacy, data misuse, bias in AI, and misinformation. Companies and IT professionals should follow clear rules, be honest, and think about how their technology affects people and the environment. Overall, ethical IT helps keep users safe and builds trust in technology.
